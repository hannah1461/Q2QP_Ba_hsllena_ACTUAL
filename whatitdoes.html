Further Information

Tom Hiddleston, the British actor known for his roles in movies like the Marvel Cinematic Universe as Loki, has been involved with UNICEF, the United Nations International Children's Emergency Fund, for several years. His involvement primarily focuses on advocating for children's rights and supporting various UNICEF initiatives.

He has made field visits to witness firsthand the conditions that children in disadvantaged communities face, aiming to raise awareness about the challenges children encounter and the importance of UNICEF's work in providing assistance and support.

Utilizing his platform as a celebrity, Hiddleston advocates for children's rights and raises awareness about issues such as child poverty, access to education, healthcare, and protection from violence and exploitation, often speaking at events and using social media to amplify UNICEF's messages and campaigns.

He supports UNICEF's emergency relief efforts by raising funds, mobilizing support, and drawing attention to the urgent needs of affected children during humanitarian crises such as natural disasters, conflicts, and epidemics.
