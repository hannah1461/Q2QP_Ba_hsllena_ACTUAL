UNICEF UK and Tom Hiddleston

Background

Tom Hiddleston is a British actor who is most well known for his role as Thor's antagonistic brother "Loki" from the Marvel Universe. Hiddleston is a member of numerous charity organizations. He is the ambassador of UNICEF UK. The organization saves children's lives with vaccines, clean water, and therepeautic food.

What Tom Hiddleston Has Done

Hiddleston used his money and Hollywood name to good use. Through his involvement in UNICEF, he has done numerous fundraising campaigns, field visits, public advocacies, and emergency relief efforts. Just recently, in the summer of 2022, Hiddleston helped raised 15 million dollars for the organization.
