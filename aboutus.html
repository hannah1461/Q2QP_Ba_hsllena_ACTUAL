Who are we?

UNICEF UK, also known as the United Kingdom Committee for UNICEF, is one of 36 UNICEF national committees based in industrialised countries. The national committees raise funds for the organisation's worldwide emergency and development work.

How can you help?

You can help UNICEF by donating money, organizing fundraisers, advocating for children's rights, volunteering your time, and purchasing UNICEF products. Your support enables UNICEF to provide essential services and protection to children worldwide, ensuring their well-being and rights are safeguarded.
